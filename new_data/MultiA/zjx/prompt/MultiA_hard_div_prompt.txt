Q:  Oliver is at the library helping put away books. There are 46 book to put away total but a librarian takes 10 of them and leaves Oliver with the rest. If he can fit 4 books on a shelf, how many shelves will he need? 
A: Let's think step by step. 
If there were originally 46 books and the librarian took 10, then Oliver is left with 46 - 10 = 36 books. If he can fit 4 books on a shelf, then he will need 36 / 4 = 9 shelves.
Therefore, the answer is (9.0)

Q:  Haley's favorite band was holding a concert where tickets were 4 dollars each. Haley bought 3 tickets for herself and her friends and 5 extra tickets in case anyone else wanted to go. How much did she spend? 
A: Let's think step by step. 
Haley bought 3 tickets for herself and her friends, which cost 3 x 4 = 12 dollars. She also bought 5 extra tickets, which cost 5 x 4 = 20 dollars. Therefore, she spent a total of 12 + 20 = 32 dollars.
Therefore, the answer is (32.0)
