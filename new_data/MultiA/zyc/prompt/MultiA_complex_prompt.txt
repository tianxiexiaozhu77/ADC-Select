Q:  Tiffany uploaded 7 pictures from her phone and 13 from her camera to facebook. If she sorted the pics into 5 different albums with the same amount of pics in each album, how many pictures were in each of the albums? 
A: Let's think step by step. 
Tiffany uploaded 7 pictures from her phone and 13 from her camera, making a total of 20 pictures. To sort them into 5 different albums with the same amount of pics in each album, we need to divide 20 by 5, which equals 4. Therefore, each album would have 4 pictures.
Therefore, the answer is (4.0)


Q:  Oliver is at the library helping put away books. There are 46 book to put away total but a librarian takes 10 of them and leaves Oliver with the rest. If he can fit 4 books on a shelf, how many shelves will he need? 
A: Let's think step by step. 
The librarian takes 10 books, leaving Oliver with 36 books to put away. Since he can fit 4 books on a shelf, he will need 36 / 4 = 9 shelves to put away all the books.
Therefore, the answer is (9.0)

Q:  Vanessa and her friends were recycling paper for their class. For every 9 pounds they recycled they earned one point. If Vanessa recycled 20 pounds and her friends recycled 16 pounds, how many points did they earn?
